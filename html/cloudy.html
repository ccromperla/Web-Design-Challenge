Latitude Vs. Cloudiness Plot

This plot represents the latitude of each city and the cloudiness in percent. Cloudiness is the measure of clouds in the sky. 100% cloudiness is an overcast day and a 0% cloudiness day is a clear day. This plot also reveals that latitude doesn't have a stong affect on cloud coverage.

One thing to further consider is the possible relationship between humidity and cloudiness. Is cloudiness determined by humidity levels?
