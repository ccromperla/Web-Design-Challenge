Latitude Vs. Max Temperature Plot

This plot represents the latitude of each city and the temperature in fahrenheit. What can be observed is that the weather does get warmer the closer one gets to the equator. Suggesting that the closer one is to the equator one wil experience warmer weather.

Another thing to note is that the weather in the Southern Hemisphere was warmer than the Northern Hemisphere. This could be due to the fact that the Northern Hemisphere in in the Winter Season and the Southern in their Summer given the date time of when this data was plotted.
